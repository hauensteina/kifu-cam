Stored Information<br>
====================<br>
<br>
The Application extracts the board position from a photo of a Go board.<br>
Go, also known as Baduk or Weiqi, is an ancient asian strategy game.<br>
<br>
The app does not track users or user behavior. There is no login, and you are entirely anonymous.<br>
<br>
However, the app does anonymously upload the pictures you take to a secured AWS S3 bucket to improve the<br>
algorithm. No link between the image and a user is stored.<br>
